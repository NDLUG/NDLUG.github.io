Activities Fair 2023

2023-09-02
by Peter Bui

On Tuesday, August 29, 2023, officers tinjamin, Octavius, and lychee greeted Notre Dame and Saint Mary‚Äôs students interested in the Notre Dame Linux Users Group at the SAO Activities Night held in the Notre Dame Stadium.

The friendly and welcoming officers had a poster designed by president alkie and passed out flyers and stickers to anyone who wanted to know more about the club.

On the flyers was a QR Code to an online interest form students could fill out to express interest in joining the club and receiving an email about our first meeting. So far, 29 people have filled out the form!

If you missed the officers at the SAO Activities Night and are interested in participating in the Notre Dame Linux Users Group, please feel free to join the other 29 students and fill out the interest form.

Our first meeting will be an Internship / Career Panel on Wednesday, September 13, 2023 at 6:30 PM in 356A Fitzpatrick Hall. More details about the meeting will be shared soon.

Be sure to follow us on @ndlug@social.ndlug.org or @nd_lug to get the latest news and updates about the Notre Dame Linux Users Group!
